** Adaptation and Mitigation
*** Refer to measures (actions, decisions, regulations, etc.) to reduce CC *damages*
**** Examples of *Mitigation* measures
***** Reduce GHG emissions of production
***** Reduce GHG emissions of consumption
***** Remove GHG from the atmosphere
***** Temporarily cool down the atmosphere (Solar Radiation Management (SRM), etc.)
**** Examples of *Adaptation* measures
***** Modify crops
***** Build dykes 
***** Make cities more resilient to heat waves, water shortages, etc.


*** Implementing measures to reduce damages
**** Specifying a damage *goal* is not enough
**** Many socio-economomic path can achieve (miss) a given goal
**** One needs to put preferences on socio-economomic paths
**** These lead to optimal balances between mitigation and adaptation (MOO!)

**** Narrow range of outcomes/risks (deterministic modelling)

*** Modelling assumptions
**** Production (GDP)
***** Non-controversial ([[https://www3.nd.edu/~nmark/Climate/DICE-simplified_2019.pdf][DICE simplified 2019]])
***** Production increases with technological efficiency
***** Production increases with capital
***** Production increases with labour
***** Definition (Cobb-Douglas production function)
****** production : TechEff -> Capital -> Labor -> Production
****** production A K L = A * K^γ * L^(1 - γ) where
******* γ (elasticity of capital, see [[Constant parameters][Constant parameters]])
***** The production over [time k, time (k + 1)] is
****** Y μ s x₀ t = production Aₜ Kₜ Lₜ where
******* Aₜ = A t (technological efficiency, see [[Time-dependent parameters][Time-dependent parameters]])
******* Kₜ = K μ s x₀ t (capital, see [[Difference equations][Difference equations]])
******* Lₜ = L t (labor, see [[Time-dependent parameters][Time-dependent parameters]])
**** Production available for investment or consumption
***** Production available increases with production
***** Production available decreases with abatements (emissions reduction)
***** Production available decreases with (climate change) damages
***** The production available over [time k, time (k + 1)] is
****** Y' μ s x₀ t = (1 - ψₜ * μₜᶿ - ξ * Hₜ²) * Yₜ where
******* ψₜ = ψ t (WTF?, see [[Time-dependent parameters][Time-dependent parameters]])
******* μₜ = μ t (mitigation policy)
******* θ  (WTF? 1 < θ) 
******* ξ  (WTF?)
******* Hₜ = H μ s x₀ t (global temperature increase, see [[Difference equations][Difference equations]])
******* Yₜ = Y μ s x₀ t (production, see [[Modelling assumptions][Modelling assumptions]])
**** Investments
***** The investments over [time k, time (k + 1)] are 
****** I μ s x₀ t = (s t) * (Y' μ s x₀ t)
**** Consumption
***** The total consumption over [time k, time (k + 1)] is
****** C μ s x₀ t = (1 - s t) * (Y' μ s x₀ t)
**** Utility of per capita consumption
***** The utility of per capita consumption is
****** U : PerCapitaConsumption -> Utility
****** U x = (x^(1 - α) - 1) / (1 - α) where
******* α is the elasticity of capital, see [[Constant parameters][Constant parameters]]
**** Welfare
***** The welfare is the product of the labor times the utility of per capita consumption
***** The welfare over [time k, time (k + 1)] is
****** W μ s x₀ t = Lₜ * U (Cₜ / Lₜ) where
******* Lₜ = L t (labor, see [[Time-dependent parameters][Time-dependent parameters]])
******* Cₜ = C μ s x₀ t (consumption, see [[Modelling assumptions][Modelling assumptions]])
**** CO₂ emissions
***** Non-controversial ([[https://www3.nd.edu/~nmark/Climate/DICE-simplified_2019.pdf][DICE simplified 2019]])
***** CO₂ emissions are the sum of non-industrial and industrial emissions
****** Non-industrial emissions are exogenous, see [[Time-dependent parameters][Time-dependent parameters]]
****** Industrial emissions are proportional to production
***** Definition
****** emissions : Production -> ℝ -> MitigationRate -> Emission -> Emission
****** emissions Y σ μ E⁰ = σ * (1 - μ) * Y + E⁰ where
***** The CO₂ emissions over [time k, time (k + 1)] are
****** E μ s x₀ t = emissions Yₜ σₜ μₜ E⁰ₜ where
******* Yₜ = Y Aₜ Kₜ Lₜ where
******** Aₜ = A t (technological efficiency, see [[Time-dependent parameters][Time-dependent parameters]])
******** Kₜ = K μ s x₀ t (capital, see [[Difference equations][Difference equations]])
******** Lₜ = L t (labor, see [[Time-dependent parameters][Time-dependent parameters]])
******* σₜ  = σ t (emissions-production ratio, see [[Time-dependent parameters][Time-dependent parameters]])
******* μₜ  = μ t (mitigation policy)
******* E⁰ₜ = E⁰ t (non-industial total CO₂ emissions, see [[Time-dependent parameters][Time-dependent parameters]])
